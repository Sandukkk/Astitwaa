Unleashing abilities of Autism

An app with a training- based approach to develop life-skills and impart education. We aim to understand the interest of people and make them capable of doing basic tasks independently.

How we work

An app for children with Autism to learn daily life skills, learn new things in a fun way through games, and other exciting ways, plan your day and get recommendations for next activity.

Parents can worry less for their child, let them have fun while they learn, and trust us as we consult professionals to validate our training methods. Your feedbacks are highly encouraged, so remember to suggest what you like us to add!
